Style Guide

This website follows a modern and clean style guide to ensure readability and engagement:

  • Typography: The Poppins font is used throughout for its modern and clean look.
  • Colors: The color palette includes shades of blue (#508fb9) for highlights and #303030 for background elements, with white (#f9f9f9) for content areas.
  • Layout: The site is laid out with a flexible grid to ensure responsiveness across devices.

Design Rationale

The design of this website is centered on providing a clean and intuitive user experience. Key design decisions were influenced by the need to highlight data in an accessible way:

  • The use of contrasting colors ensures that key information stands out without overwhelming the user.
  • Typography choices balance readability with aesthetics, ensuring that text-heavy sections are easy to scan.
  • The grid layout provides flexibility, allowing for smooth responsiveness on different screen sizes.
